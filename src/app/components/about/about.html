About Us

Notestalgia is more than notes — it’s a space where learners discover, creators share, and knowledge flows both ways.

Our Mission

At Notestalgia, we aim to make studying smarter and more accessible by providing curated handwritten and digital notes from real students. Our goal is to support learners with quality resources that save time, enhance understanding, and reduce academic pressure.

We also strive to empower note creators by giving them a platform to share their knowledge and earn from it. Through this, we’re building a helpful, supportive learning community where efforts are appreciated and education becomes collaborative.
